Types of Greenhouse

GLASSHOUSE: It is also known as glasshouse. It has structure with walls and roof made chiefly to transparent material, such as glass.

LEANTO: It shares a wall with a building and relies on the building structure toprovide some support for the Greenhouse roof.

EVEN SPAN: It is of standard type and full-size structure. It has two roof slopes are of equal pitch and width.

UNEVEN SPAN: It is an uneven pitches and widths. Use of this style is limited to hillsides. Modern greenhouse are build on level ground.

RIDGE AND FURROW : It is composed of a number of greenhouses connected one another along the length of the eave. The eave serves as furrow to carry rain and melted snow away

QUONSET : It look like an upside down half circle. Frame is made of laminated wood or bent pipe
